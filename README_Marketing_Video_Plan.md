# Methsara Publications - IE2091 Marketing Video Plan

## Guidelines Checklist
- [ ] Good audio quality (No noise, use background music gracefully).
- [ ] No code or complex architecture diagrams.
- [ ] Understandable by a non-technical audience.
- [ ] Highlight key points with neat text overlays.
- [ ] Short, engaging clips with smooth transitions.

---

## Storyboard & Script Outline

### 1. Grab Attention (0:00 - 0:15)
- **Visuals**: A frustrated bookstore manager sitting amidst piles of educational books and mixed-up paper receipts.
- **Text Overlay**: "Running a modern bookstore shouldn't feel this chaotic."
- **Audio/Voiceover**: "Ever struggled to track thousands of educational books, multiple suppliers, and missing customer orders all at the same time?"

### 2. Problem Statement (0:15 - 0:30)
- **Visuals**: Split-screen showing out-of-stock shelves, angry customers, and manual ledger entries. 
- **Voiceover**: "For publishers like Methsara Publications, relying on manual processes means delayed orders, inaccurate inventory, and lost customer loyalty. It’s hard to grow when you’re stuck managing paperwork."

### 3. Solution (Your Product) (0:30 - 0:50)
- **Visuals**: Logo animation of Methsara Publications Webstore. Transition to clean, fast screen recordings of the customized UI Dashboards.
- **Voiceover**: "Enter the Methsara Publications Webstore platform. A fully centralized, digital ecosystem seamlessly integrating every aspect of book publishing and sales."

### 4. Value Proposition (0:50 - 1:10)
- **Visuals**: Show quick pop-ups or icons highlighting: `Real-time Inventory`, `Automated Suppliers`, and `Smart Promotions`.
- **Text Overlay**: Faster. Smarter. Seamless.
- **Voiceover**: "With our platform, you eliminate manual errors. Manage user permissions securely, track complex inventories in real-time, and trigger supplier restocking instantly. It’s faster, cheaper, and smarter."

### 5. Demo / Use Case (1:10 - 2:00)
*Keep it very fast and simple, showing high-level user flow.*
- **Visual 1 (Product Manager)**: Quickly adding a new 'Physics WorkBook' with an image upload.
- **Visual 2 (Marketing)**: Using the Date Picker to create a "BACKTOSCHOOL" coupon code.
- **Visual 3 (Customer)**: A customer adds the book to the cart, applies the coupon, and places the order.
- **Visual 4 (Finance & Inventory)**: Dashboard instantly updating sales revenue and dropping the stock count by 1.
- **Voiceover**: "Watch as a newly added textbook receives a custom promotion, is instantly purchased by a customer, and effortlessly updates both our financial ledgers and stock levels—all in just a few clicks."

### 6. Closing & Call to Action (2:00 - 2:15)
- **Visuals**: The team members (or just their names/roles along with the 6 Epics), fading into the Methsara Publications Logo.
- **Voiceover**: "Methsara Publications. Empowering Education through Seamless Management."
- **Text Overlay**: "Smarter Book Management, One Click Away."
