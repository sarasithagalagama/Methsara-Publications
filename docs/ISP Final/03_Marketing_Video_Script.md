# Marketing Video Script - Methsara Publications Webstore

This is the complete recording script and production plan for the final marketing video.

## Target Runtime

- Preferred: 3 minutes 30 seconds
- Allowed range: follow module guideline in `IE2091 Marketing Video Guidelines.pdf`

## Video Objective

Show the platform as a practical, production-ready digital solution for educational publishing operations, covering both customer value and internal operational value.

---

## Final Storyboard and Voiceover

### Scene 1 - Opening Problem (0:00-0:20)

Visual sequence:
- Quick shots: paper logs, manual stock notes, delayed confirmation calls.
- Fade into dark-themed dashboard UI.

Voiceover:
"Educational bookstores operating across multiple branches often struggle with delayed stock updates, fragmented supplier coordination, and limited customer reach."

On-screen text:
- "Manual workflows"
- "Limited visibility"
- "Operational delays"

### Scene 2 - Product Introduction (0:20-0:45)

Visual sequence:
- Dashboard header, navigation, and feature cards.
- Hero title card with project name.

Voiceover:
"Methsara Publications Webstore is a full-stack digital platform built to unify products, orders, suppliers, inventory, and promotions in one secure role-based system."

On-screen text:
- "MERN Architecture"
- "Role-Based Access"
- "Real-Time Operations"

### Scene 3 - Customer Journey (0:45-1:20)

Visual sequence:
- Product browse page -> filter/search -> product detail.
- Add to cart -> checkout -> order confirmation.

Voiceover:
"Customers can discover books by grade and subject, check detailed product information, and complete checkout smoothly using cash on delivery or bank transfer proof upload."

On-screen text:
- "Smart Search and Filters"
- "Fast Checkout"
- "Order Tracking"

### Scene 4 - Role-Based Management (1:20-1:55)

Visual sequence:
- Admin dashboard.
- Product manager dashboard.
- Finance dashboard.

Voiceover:
"Internal teams operate through role-specific dashboards. Administrators manage access, product managers maintain catalog quality, and finance managers monitor transactions and revenue."

On-screen text:
- "Admin"
- "Product"
- "Finance"

### Scene 5 - Supplier and Inventory Operations (1:55-2:35)

Visual sequence:
- Purchase order creation.
- Low stock alert page.
- Transfer request and approval flow.

Voiceover:
"The platform links supplier workflows with inventory control. Teams can create purchase orders, monitor low-stock alerts, and perform controlled branch-to-branch stock transfers with approval checks."

On-screen text:
- "PO and SO Workflows"
- "Low-Stock Alerts"
- "Transfer Approvals"

### Scene 6 - Marketing and Loyalty (2:35-3:00)

Visual sequence:
- Coupon/campaign management screens.
- Gift voucher page and checkout discount display.

Voiceover:
"Marketing teams launch campaigns, manage coupon rules, and issue gift vouchers, enabling targeted promotions and stronger customer retention."

On-screen text:
- "Campaigns"
- "Coupons"
- "Gift Vouchers"

### Scene 7 - Closing Value Statement (3:00-3:30)

Visual sequence:
- Montage of dashboards and checkout flow.
- End card with team credits.

Voiceover:
"Methsara Publications Webstore transforms a traditional business into a connected digital ecosystem, improving operational speed, visibility, and customer experience across every branch."

On-screen text:
- "Digital Transformation in Action"

## Shot Checklist (Capture Plan)

- Login as Admin (role-based access proof).
- Product browse/filter/detail flow.
- Cart and checkout sequence.
- Finance summary view.
- Supplier order status flow.
- Inventory low-stock and stock transfer flow.
- Marketing coupon/campaign and gift voucher flow.

---

## Editing Plan

- Resolution: 1920x1080.
- Frame rate: 30 fps.
- Subtitle style: high-contrast white text with dark shadow.
- Background music: low-volume instrumental, no vocal conflict with narration.
- Transitions: clean cut or short fade only.
- Keep all overlays under 7 words per line when possible.

---

## Audio Script Notes

- Record in a quiet environment.
- Maintain steady pace and neutral accent.
- Pause briefly between scenes for easier subtitle sync.
- Normalize final audio to consistent loudness.

---

## Compliance Checklist Before Export

- No credentials or tokens visible.
- No personal user data visible.
- UI text is readable at 720p and above.
- Runtime is within module requirement.
- Final export tested on desktop and mobile playback.
